Funnel #1: The Quiz Funnel

Why It Works

Quizzes are the secret weapon for dating brands. They offer value upfront while naturally qualifying leads based on their answers.

The Psychology Behind Quiz Success

  • Curiosity Gap: People need to know their results
  • Personalization: Answers create custom follow-up sequences
  • Low Commitment: Easier yes than "Download My Guide"
  • Data Collection: Learn about your audience at scale

Funnel #2: The Webinar Funnel

Why It Works

Webinars build trust at scale. They position you as the expert while giving massive value before any pitch.

The Trust Acceleration Formula

  • Authority: 60-90 minutes of pure expertise
  • Social Proof: Live attendees create community
  • Urgency: Limited time offers feel natural
  • Education: They learn while you sell

Funnel #3: The High-Ticket Funnel

Why It Works

Not everyone is ready for your $3K-$10K program immediately. This funnel nurtures them until they are.

  • Free Content: Build initial trust

  • Mid Ticket ($497-$997): Deeper transformation
  • High Ticket ($3K-$10K): Complete system + support
